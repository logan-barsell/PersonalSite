About Me

Hello! My name is Logan Barsell, a 23 year old musician and web designer from the San Francisco Bay Area. Music has been my passion since I was a young kid. At 9 years old I picked up my first drum stick, and a few years later I began strumming chords on guitar and trying to sing my favorite songs. Throughout high school I was in and out of several bands, but by the time I graduated my attention was mainly directed towards a solo project I call Little Hollow. This is where I can express the spiritual, more down to earth side of myself that is rooted in a pursuit of self-discovery, spreading peace and love, and giving messages of positivity. It wasn't too long, however, before I started to miss being in a band. Around this same time, my twin brother's band started seeking out a rhythm guitarist.
